Who are we and what do we do?

We are a multinational airline company headquartered in the city of Split, specifically the Pujanke district. We specialize in inexpensive travel, specifically between countries belonging to the Schengen Area.

Why use us?

We offer some of the cheapest flights in the European Union, with a focus on punctuality and safety. Since our founding we haven't had a single flight-related accident, nor customer complaint about comfort. Our flights are never delayed or canceled, we have never had late arrivals. Our staff are trained professionals who will gladly assist you with anything to ensure you have a comfortable flight.

What is our scope of operations?

Currently we only offer domestic flights between countries inside the European Union, or more specifically, belonging to the Schengen Area. However, we plan to expand our operations to all European countries in the near future. We also plan to introduce a few intercontinental flights, namely between Europe and the US, but this is still only in the planning phase.

What is our refund policy?

We offer 50% refunds on all flights up to 72 hours before departure. This includes any amenities purchased alongside the flight
