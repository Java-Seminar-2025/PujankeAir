Let's get started

To search for flights and reserve them, go to 'FLIGHTS'. 'MY BOOKINGS' is where your reserved and paid-for flights reside. To learn more about us, go to 'ABOUT US'. To set up/manage your account, click on your username on the navbar. If you're not already logged in, you can do so by clicking on 'LOG IN' on the navbar (note: this is necessary to use the booking system).
